    Bruno Miguel Borges Fernandes is a professional Portuguese footballer who plays for the English giants, Manchester United.

    Bruno Fernandes Biography

    Bruno Miguel Borges Fernandes, popularly referred to as Bruno Fernandes is a Portuguese midfielder.

    He made his professional career debut in 2012 when he joined Novara. His stay there was only for a season after making 23 appearances and scoring only 4 goals.

    Bruno moved to Udinese in the 2013-2014 season and made a total number of appearances 95 and scored 11 goals within his 3 years stay there.

    Bruno Fernandes joined Sampdoria from Udinese and left there after a year’s stay joining Sporting CP.

    After joining Sporting CP in 2017, he managed to catch the eyes of the Premier League giants, Manchester United.

    He made his dream move to Manchester United in 2019-2020 where he made 22 appearances in his first season and scored 12 goals.

    He has now made a sum 146 appearances and scored 53 goals in his stay at Manchester United so far.

    Bruno Fernandes made his International debut in 2017 and has since then made 50 appearances scoring 11 goals for the Portuguese National Team.
