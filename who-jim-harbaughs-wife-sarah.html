    Who is Jim Harbaugh's wife, Sarah?

    Born on September 20, 1978, in Belton, Missouri, Sarah Harbaugh is the second wife of veteran coach Jim Harbaugh.

    The pair met in Las Vegas after Jim's divorce from his first wife in 2006.

    Subsequently, they tied the knot on January 5, 2008.

    The couple share four children together - two daughters named Addison and Katherine, and two sons named Jack and John.

    Sarah has been known for doing charity work locally and internationally in countries such as Peru.

    Who is Jim Harbaugh?

    Born on December 23, 1963, in Toledo, Ohio, Jim Harbaugh is a veteran football coach and a former quarterback.

    As of the time of writing, he is the 20th head football coach of the Michigan Wolverines.

    Prior to coaching the Wolverines, he coached the Oakland Raiders from 2002 to 2003, the San Diego Toreros from 2004 to 2006, the Stanford Cardinal from 2007 to 2010, and the San Francisco 49ners from 2011 to 2014, before joining Michigan in 2015.
