    About

    English-born actor who most famously played Major Arnold Ernst Toht, the deranged Nazi, in the 1981 film, Raiders of the Lost Ark. He also played Heinrich Himmler in Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade.

    Before Fame

    He trained briefly at the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art and went on to perform in a 1962 Royal Court Theatre production of Chips with Everything.
