Application deadline nears for energy credit help | News, Sports, Jobs

28 Jun 2024, 00:00

The Maui News The deadline to apply for federally funded energy credit assistance for low-income residents who are not in crisis but require help to pay their heating and cooling bills is 4:30 p.m. Thursday, Maui Economic Opportunity announced. MEO administers the popular once-a-year Hawaii Low-Income Home Energy Assistance Program, also known as LIHEAP, in Maui County. LIHEAP provides a credit on utility accounts of income-eligible households. Credit amounts are based on income and number of people in the household, as well as the number of applicants, with the amount determined by the state, according to a MEO news release.
